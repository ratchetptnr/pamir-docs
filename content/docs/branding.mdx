## Usage Guidelines

### Do

- Use the logo with adequate clear space (minimum 1x the height of the "p")
- Maintain aspect ratio when scaling
- Use the appropriate color variant for the background

### Don't

- Stretch or distort the logo
- Apply drop shadows, gradients, or effects
- Place the light logo on light backgrounds (or vice versa)
- Recreate the logo with different fonts
