## QR Code Issues

<Accordions>

<Accordion title="QR code won't scan">

**On Device:** QR code is displayed
**On Phone:** Camera doesn't detect it or nothing happens when pointing at it

**Solutions:**

1. **Check camera focus**
   - Make sure camera is focused on the QR code
   - Hold phone steady for 2-3 seconds
   - Try moving phone closer or farther away (6-12 inches is ideal)

2. **Improve lighting**
   - QR codes need good lighting to scan
   - Avoid glare on the device screen
   - Try scanning in a brighter location

3. **Clean the device screen**
   - Wipe screen with soft cloth
   - Remove any protective film that might be on screen

4. **Try a different camera app**
   - Some phones have issues with built-in camera
   - Download a QR scanner app from app store
   - Use the QR scanner app instead

5. **Manual workaround**
   - Download the Distiller app directly from app store
   - Open the app
   - Tap "Set up new device"
   - App will search for your Distiller automatically

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="QR code opens wrong link or app">

**What's happening:** The QR code should open the Distiller app, but something else opens instead.

**Solutions:**

1. **Clear the QR detection**
   - Point camera away from QR code
   - Close the notification that appeared
   - Try scanning again

2. **Download app manually**
   - Search for "Distiller by Pamir" in your app store
   - Download and install
   - Open the app manually
   - Tap "Set up new device"

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Device screen is black (no QR code)">

**On Device:** Screen is completely black or blank

**Solutions:**

1. **Check power**
   - Is the device plugged in?
   - Is the power outlet working? (test with another device)
   - Try a different power cable if available

2. **Wait for boot**
   - Device takes 10-15 seconds to boot after power on
   - Screen will show "Starting..." then the QR code
   - Be patient

3. **Restart the device**
   - Unplug the device
   - Wait 10 seconds
   - Plug it back in
   - Wait for QR code to appear

4. **Factory reset (last resort)**
   - Unplug the device for 10 seconds
   - Plug it back in
   - Device will boot into factory reset mode
   - Follow on-screen instructions

</Accordion>

</Accordions>

## Bluetooth Issues

<Accordions>

<Accordion title="Device not found in Bluetooth list">

**On Phone:** App shows "Searching for devices..." but never finds your Distiller

**Solutions:**

1. **Check distance**
   - Move phone within 3 feet (1 meter) of device
   - Bluetooth has limited range

2. **Enable Bluetooth on phone**
   - Go to phone Settings → Bluetooth
   - Make sure Bluetooth is ON
   - Return to Distiller app

3. **Check device is in pairing mode**
   - Device screen should show "No Internet - Scan QR"
   - If it shows something else, restart the device

4. **Restart Bluetooth on phone**
   - Turn Bluetooth OFF
   - Wait 5 seconds
   - Turn Bluetooth ON
   - Tap "Scan Again" in app

5. **Restart both devices**
   - Close the app completely
   - Restart your phone
   - Restart the Distiller (unplug and plug back in)
   - Try setup again

6. **Check for interference**
   - Move away from:
     - Other Bluetooth devices
     - Microwave ovens
     - WiFi routers (temporarily)
   - These can interfere with Bluetooth signal

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Wrong PIN error">

**On Phone:** "Incorrect PIN" message after entering the PIN

**On Device:** Shows a PIN (e.g., "PIN: 1234")

**Solutions:**

1. **Check the PIN again**
   - Look carefully at device screen
   - Make sure you're reading it correctly
   - Easy to confuse: 0 and O, 1 and I, 5 and S

2. **Wait and try again**
   - Device may generate a new PIN after failed attempts
   - Check device screen for updated PIN
   - Enter the NEW PIN shown

3. **Restart pairing**
   - Tap "Cancel" in app
   - Tap your device again in the list
   - Device will show a new PIN
   - Enter the new PIN

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Multiple devices showing up - which is mine?">

**On Phone:** App shows several Distillers (e.g., "Distiller-A4F2", "Distiller-B8C9")

**Solutions:**

1. **Check signal strength**
   - The device with the MOST bars is usually yours
   - It's the closest one

2. **Check serial number**
   - Look on the back/bottom of your device
   - Find the serial number (e.g., "A4F2")
   - Match it to the device name in the list

3. **Move away from other devices**
   - If you're in an office/lab with multiple Distillers
   - Move to a different room temporarily
   - Only your device should appear

4. **Try connecting anyway**
   - If you connect to the wrong device:
     - You won't know the PIN
     - Or the owner will have set a password
   - Just cancel and try a different device

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Bluetooth pairing keeps failing">

**On Phone:** Connection fails repeatedly, even with correct PIN

**Solutions:**

1. **Forget previous pairings**
   - Go to phone Settings → Bluetooth
   - Look for your Distiller in "My Devices"
   - Tap (i) icon next to it
   - Tap "Forget This Device"
   - Go back to app and try again

2. **Restart both devices**
   - Close the app
   - Turn Bluetooth OFF on phone
   - Restart the Distiller (unplug and plug back in)
   - Turn Bluetooth back ON
   - Open app and try again

3. **Check phone OS version**
   - Make sure your phone OS is up to date
   - iOS: Settings → General → Software Update
   - Android: Settings → System → System Update

</Accordion>

</Accordions>

## Password and Security Issues

<Accordions>

<Accordion title="Password requirements not clear">

**On Phone:** Can't proceed because password doesn't meet requirements

**Requirements:**
- Minimum 6 characters
- Can include letters, numbers, and symbols
- No maximum length

**Tips:**
- Use something memorable but secure
- Write it down temporarily (you'll need it later)
- Don't use "123456" or "password"

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Passwords don't match">

**On Phone:** "Passwords don't match" error

**Solution:**
- Re-enter both fields carefully
- Use "Show password" checkbox to verify
- Make sure no extra spaces

</Accordion>

</Accordions>

## WiFi Connection Issues

<Accordions>

<Accordion title="My WiFi network isn't listed">

**On Phone:** Your network doesn't appear in the WiFi list

**Solutions:**

1. **Check WiFi frequency**
   - **Distiller supports both 2.4GHz and 5GHz WiFi**
   - Make sure your network is broadcasting (SSID visible)
   - Check your router settings to confirm WiFi is enabled

2. **Check router settings**
   - Is WiFi enabled on your router?
   - Is SSID broadcast enabled? (network should be visible)
   - Router must broadcast the network name

3. **Move closer to router**
   - Weak signal might not be detected
   - Move device within 15 feet of router
   - Tap "Scan Again" in app

4. **Enter network manually**
   - Tap "Network not listed?" in app
   - Enter your network name (SSID) exactly
   - Make sure it's spelled correctly (case-sensitive)
   - Enter password
   - Tap Connect

5. **Restart router**
   - Unplug router
   - Wait 30 seconds
   - Plug back in
   - Wait 2 minutes for full boot
   - Tap "Scan Again" in app

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="WiFi password incorrect">

**On Device:** Shows "Connecting..." then goes back to "No Internet"
**On Phone:** "Password incorrect" or "Authentication failed"

**Solutions:**

1. **Double-check password**
   - WiFi passwords are case-sensitive
   - Check for typos
   - Use "Show password" to verify

2. **Common password mistakes**
   - Confusing: 0 (zero) and O (letter)
   - Confusing: 1 (one) and l (lowercase L)
   - Extra space at beginning or end
   - Using old password (did you recently change it?)

3. **Copy password from router settings**
   - Log into your router admin page
   - Find WiFi password
   - Copy it exactly
   - Paste into app

4. **Try a different device first**
   - Try connecting your phone to the same WiFi
   - If phone can't connect, the password is wrong
   - If phone connects, check for typos when entering in app

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="WiFi connected but no internet">

**On Device:** Shows "WiFi Connected, No Internet"
**On Phone:** Shows "Connected to WiFi but can't reach internet"

**What this means:** Device joined the WiFi network successfully, but there's no internet connection.

**Solutions:**

1. **Check your internet connection**
   - Can other devices access internet?
   - Try browsing a website on your phone (using WiFi, not cellular)
   - If nothing works, your internet is down

2. **Router or ISP issue**
   - Restart your router:
     - Unplug it
     - Wait 30 seconds
     - Plug back in
     - Wait 2 minutes
   - Check with ISP if internet is down in your area

3. **Captive portal (hotels, airports, cafes)**
   - Some networks require login via web browser
   - Distiller can't handle captive portals yet
   - Try a different network or set up a hotspot from your phone

4. **Firewall blocking device**
   - Corporate or school networks may block IoT devices
   - Contact network administrator
   - Or use a different network (home network, mobile hotspot)

5. **Wait and retry**
   - Sometimes internet takes a few minutes to come back
   - Tap "Try Again" after a minute
   - Or connect to a different network

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Connection times out">

**On Device:** Shows "Connecting..." for a long time (over 1 minute), then fails

**Solutions:**

1. **Check WiFi signal strength**
   - In the network list, check the bars next to your network
   - If weak (1-2 bars), move device closer to router
   - Try to get at least 3 bars

2. **Router is overloaded**
   - Too many devices connected
   - Restart router
   - Disconnect some devices temporarily

3. **Network congestion**
   - Try setup at a different time (less congestion)
   - Or use a different network

4. **Change WiFi channel**
   - Log into router settings
   - Change WiFi channel (try 1, 6, or 11 for 2.4GHz)
   - Reduces interference from neighbors' WiFi

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Connection works but then fails after a few seconds">

**On Device:** Shows "Connected" briefly, then goes back to "No Internet"

**Solutions:**

1. **Router DHCP issues**
   - Router may be out of IP addresses
   - Restart router
   - Or manually assign IP address in router settings

2. **MAC address filtering**
   - Some routers only allow known devices
   - Check router settings
   - Add Distiller's MAC address to allowed list
   - (MAC address is shown on device back/bottom)

3. **Network security settings**
   - Some enterprise/school networks block new devices
   - Contact network administrator
   - Or use a home network

</Accordion>

</Accordions>

## App Issues

<Accordions>

<Accordion title="App crashes during setup">

**What happens:** App closes unexpectedly during setup

**Good news:** You can continue where you left off!

**Solutions:**

1. **Before you set device password:**
   - Reopen app
   - Start setup from beginning
   - Device will show QR code again

2. **After you set device password:**
   - Reopen app
   - App will search for your device
   - Tap your device
   - Enter device password
   - Continue from WiFi setup

3. **If crashes persist:**
   - Update app to latest version
   - Restart your phone
   - Try again

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="App stuck on 'Searching for devices'">

**On Phone:** App shows "Searching..." forever, doesn't find device

**See:** [Device not found in Bluetooth list](#device-not-found-in-bluetooth-list) above

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="App won't install from App Store">

**On Phone:** Can't download or install the app

**Solutions:**

1. **Check phone storage**
   - Go to Settings → Storage
   - Make sure you have at least 100MB free

2. **Check internet connection**
   - Make sure phone has internet (WiFi or cellular)
   - Try downloading a different app to test

3. **Restart phone**
   - Turn phone off
   - Turn back on
   - Try downloading again

4. **Update phone OS**
   - App may require newer OS version
   - Update to latest version

</Accordion>

</Accordions>

## Still Stuck?

If none of these solutions work:

1. **Factory reset and try again**
   - Unplug the device for 10 seconds
   - Plug it back in to boot into factory reset
   - Confirm factory reset on device screen
   - Start setup from beginning

2. **Contact support**
   - Email: support@pamir.cloud
   - Include:
     - Device serial number
     - What step you're stuck on
     - What error messages you see
     - Photos of device screen and app screen (if possible)

3. **Check for updates**
   - Make sure you have the latest app version
   - Check for device firmware updates (Settings in app)
