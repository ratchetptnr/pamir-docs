## Connect Your Phone & Sign In

### Step 1: Scan QR Code & Sign In

**What to do on your phone:**
1. Open the Camera app
2. Point at the QR code on your Distiller's screen
3. Tap the notification to open the Distiller app
4. On the Welcome screen, tap **Continue with Apple** or **Continue with Google**

### Step 2: Search for Devices

**What happens automatically:**
- Once signed in, the app searches for your Distiller via Bluetooth
- Finds your device and shows it in the list of nearby devices

## Connect to WiFi

Your device is now linked to your account. Now it's time to get it online.

### Step 3: Select WiFi Network

**What happens:**
1. App requests WiFi network list from device via BLE
2. Device scans nearby networks and sends list back to app
3. App displays available networks

**App Display:**

<div className="flex justify-center my-8">
  <img
    src="/docs/app/04-wifi-list.png"
    alt="App showing WiFi networks - select your network"
    className="w-full max-w-[320px] rounded-2xl border shadow-xl"
  />
</div>

**What to do:**
1. Find your WiFi network in the list
2. Tap to select it

### Step 4: Enter WiFi Password & Connect

**What happens:**
1. App displays password entry screen
2. You enter your WiFi password
3. App sends credentials to device via Bluetooth
4. Device attempts to connect to WiFi

**App Display:**

<div className="flex justify-center my-8">
  <img
    src="/docs/app/06-wifi-connecting.png"
    alt="App shows connecting - device is attempting WiFi"
    className="w-full max-w-[320px] rounded-2xl border shadow-xl"
  />
</div>

**What to do:**
1. Enter your WiFi password
2. Tap "Connect"
3. Wait for the device to connect
